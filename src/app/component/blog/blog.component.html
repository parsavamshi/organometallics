Conservation of Water Bodies :

As the heavy industrial discharges and wastes quite often get discharged into rivers and other water bodies, enough concern must be directed towards avoiding such Water pollution through process of releasing harmful chemical discharges into water bodies without pre-processing filtering the dangerous substances, thereby saving the fresh water eco systems.

Conservation of Fossil fuels :

Enough care must be taken to avoid the usage and utilisation of Fossil fuels as the Energy determinants for the Industrial ventures as they deplete the planet of the natural resources balance and steps should focus on preferring alternate sources of energy.

Conservation of paper pulp:

Enough care must be instituted into minimising the Usage of paper pulp and allied plant products in all the Industrial and Official applications, thereby conserving the most precious Floral systems, an urgent need of the hour.
